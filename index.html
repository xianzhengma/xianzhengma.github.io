Xianzheng Ma(马宪政)

Hello! I am Xianzheng. Welcome!

I am currently a DPhil Student (10.2023- ) at Department of Engineering Science, University of Oxford, supervised by Prof. Victor Prisacariu.

Previously, I was a full-time researcher at Shanghai AI Lab, under the supervision of Prof. Chao Dong. I obtained my Bachelor's and Master's degrees from Wuhan University in 2018 and 2021.

My research interests include LLMs, 3D computer vision and robotics, especially using LLMs's world knowledge to enpower the 3D world understanding and interaction.

For any suggestions or collaborations, reach out to me at xianzheng@robots.ox.ac.uk.
