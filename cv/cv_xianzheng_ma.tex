%----------RESEARCH INTERESTS----------
\section{Research Interests}
3D computer vision and robotics. Previously focused on leveraging 3D Large Language Models to reason about and understand the 3D world. More recently, shifted towards enabling robots to understand and interact with the physical world through video and 3D modalities, with a current emphasis on utilizing world models to improve the generalization capability of general-purpose robotic systems.
